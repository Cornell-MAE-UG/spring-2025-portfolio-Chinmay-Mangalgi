Our group analyzed both the carburetor and the centrifugal pump; I focused on the pump portion, which included disassembling the pump to inspect the impeller, seals, and internal flow paths, then analyzing how fluid moves through the pump during typical operation. <h3>What I Did</h3>

<ul>
  <li>Disassembled the pump and identified the main internal components relevant to flow (impeller, casing, seals, and ports).</li>
  <li>Mapped fluid flow paths from inlet to outlet and related them to centrifugal pump theory.</li>
  <li>Measured port diameters to calculate specific speed and specific diameter of the pump.</li>
  <li>Summarized the pump analysis on camera as part of the final dissection and fluid mechanics explanation video.</li>
</ul>
